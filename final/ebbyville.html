Ebbyville's History

Ebbyville was orgionally setteled in 1851, and was incorporated in 1853. When the pioneers arrived at Ebbyville, there were a band of Goshute Shoshone indians who lived here as well. During the early 20th Century, a mining smelter was built in a canyon east of Ebbyville, and the Ebbyville Valley railway served as transportation to and from the mine. When World War 2 started, the US govenrment obtained 25,000 acres, and built what would be an Orddnance Depot and Army Depot. in 1993, about 1700 acres of the depot was anexted into Ebbyville.

Population (2019): 34,535

Climate: Spring, Summer, and fall are nice. Winter can be cold
